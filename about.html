Mission

Our mission is to unleash greatness in everyone through authentic martial arts under World Champions.

By creating a safe, fun, and supportive environment, Super Sonic Fight Club enables everyone to enjoy the benefits of martial arts.

Focus

Super Sonic Fight Club has the highest standards of excellence, and you can expect martial arts instruction of the highest quality available anywhere in the world. Each course at Super Sonic Fight Club has been carefully developed in order to maximize your learning in the least amount of time.

FOUNDER'S MESSAGE

On November 14, 2008, I walked into an empty room with a bare cement floor in Singapore with a few of my Muay Thai brother warriors from Thailand. Together, we labored away day and night to build the ring, to assemble the cage, to lay down the mats, to hang the heavy bags, and to decorate the little place called Super Sonic Fight Club. We had big dreams to make the world a better place – in our small and humble way. Our dream was to create a place that people could call home and a place where everyone was family. As lifelong martial artists, our dream was to share our greatest passion in life. Our dream was to touch lives and to inspire positive change. Our dream was to make dreams happen for others.

There is a saying in Thailand that when you drop a pebble into a pond, the ripples can be felt all the way to the edge of the pond. That night, we dropped the little pebble called Super Sonic Fight Club into the pond.

Here is to Unleashing Your Greatness!

With much respect,

Chatri Sityodtong
